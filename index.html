Cyberspace Solarium Commission Bot


Type some text into the box and hit enter to see how a language model based on the Cyberspace Solarium Commission's final report completes the sentence.


The softmax temperature is set to 1.0. Temperature controls how random the predicted sentence is. A temperature of 1 does not adjust the model's predictions. A higher temperature will produce more random predictions. Use the slider above to adjust the temperature.
